Candidate Journey

From first contact to successful placement - fully automated and empathetic

🎯
STEP 01

First Contact

Candidate clicks Instagram/Facebook ad and is instantly greeted by multilingual AI chatbot (EN/SI/TA) via WhatsApp or Messenger

📝
STEP 02

Pre-Screening

AI conducts friendly conversation to collect key info: height, language skills, experience, and requests CV upload - all in candidate's preferred language

⚡
STEP 03

Instant Processing

CV uploaded → OCR extracts text → GPT-4 parser structures data → Auto-acknowledgment sent → Profile created in database (all within 60 seconds)

🤖
STEP 04

Intelligent Matching

Fuzzy logic engine matches candidate to jobs with "wiggle room" - if not perfect fit, suggests alternative positions automatically

✅
STEP 05

Recruiter Review

Project handler reviews AI-screened candidate on dashboard, certifies for interview, or transfers to better-fitting project with one click

📅
STEP 06

Interview Scheduled

System auto-schedules interview → Sends trilingual WhatsApp/SMS invite → Candidate confirms → Reminder sent 24hrs before → No-shows minimized

🎉
STEP 07

Post-Interview

Recruiter updates status → If hired: onboarding begins | If not selected: moved to Future Pool with reason → Auto-remarketing when new jobs match

Key Features

Comprehensive automation that solves every recruitment challenge

🌐

Trilingual AI

Native support for English, Sinhala, and Tamil across all touchpoints - chatbots, voice calls, notifications, and forms. Automatic language detection and switching.

📞

AI Voice Receptionist

3CX-powered AI answers every call 24/7 in any language. Natural conversations, sentiment detection, and seamless handoff to humans when needed.

🤖

Smart CV Parsing

GPT-4 powered OCR extracts 20+ fields from any CV format (PDF, image, scanned docs). Handles multilingual resumes with 95%+ accuracy.

🎯

Fuzzy Matching

Intelligent matching with configurable "wiggle room" - finds candidates who are 95% fit instead of rejecting at 99%. Maximizes placement potential.

🔄

Auto Alternative Suggestions

If candidate doesn't fit Job A, system automatically searches all active positions and suggests Job B, C, or D. No candidate left behind.

📊

Unified Dashboard

30 recruiters work in one system. Kanban pipeline, certification workflow, click-to-call, WhatsApp messaging, and full conversation history - all in one place.

📧

15 Email Integration

All 15 Gmail accounts (Helpers, Workers, Drivers, etc.) automatically monitored. CVs extracted, acknowledged, and routed to correct project - zero manual sorting.

💬

Social Media Automation

Instagram/Facebook ads redirect to WhatsApp/Messenger bots. Automated screening, CV collection, and database entry - converts leads while you sleep.

🏷️

Future Pool Management

Candidates not currently placed are tagged with skills and reasons. Auto-remarketing when new jobs match. Your CV database becomes a goldmine.
